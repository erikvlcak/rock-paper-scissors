Giant snowman is a fearsome monster that can sacrifice one of his limbs to extinguish a fire. There is only one thing that can destroy him - a direct hit with a large boulder hurled from a medieval catapult.

Yes, even a small fire can destroy this mighty siege weapon, but not even the greatest snowman can withstand a giant boulder hurled in his way!
