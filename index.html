SNOWMAN - CAMPFIRE - CATAPULT

How to play
Pick one of three options, confirm your choice and Wait for the result. Winner gets 1 point. Game ends after 5 turns, whoever has more points wins the duel.
Snowman beats Campfire but is beaten by Catapult
Snowman can sacrifice a portion of his body to extinguish a Campfire, but is defenseless against a hit from a Catapult.
Campfire beats Catapult but is beaten by Snowman
Even a small Campfire is enough to set wooden Catapult ablaze. However, Snowman can easily extinguish a Campfire with melted portion of his body.
Catapult beats Snowman but is beaten by Campfire
No Snowman can withstand a direct hit by a Catapult, but if put your Catapult over a Campfire, it will quiclky turn to charcoal!
